According to Wikipedia, the bicycle also referred to as bike or cycle has been around since the 19th century. What we know as a bicycle today was originally referred to as a "safety bicycle", the original design had a large front wheel and did not have a chain.

There are about as many types of bicycles and each type is specifically designed for a specific terrain. Various other types are built for as cross between multiple types, for a specific niche, or to simply be unusual.

    Common Types of Bicycles:
  • Road
  • Stunt
  • Mountain
  • Tandem (Bicycle built for two)

Maintaining a bicycle is fairly straight forward. Professional and constant riders will have their own maintenance schedule, but for the casual rider it is a good idea to check these items every season.

    Top to Bottom Bicycle Checklist:
  1. Check the brakes are working correctly
  2. Check the shifter working (if applicable)
  3. Check the seat height is correct
  4. Lubricate and clean the chain
  5. Check the tires for damage or excessive wear

Be sure to check your helmet for damage and be sure it fits!
